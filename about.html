ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Qatrina and I am an artist. This website is me sharing some of the materials I use to create art and to display some of my work. This aims to help those who visit begin somewhere in their art journey and to motivate them to start creating art no matter the tools you use. As someone who aspires to be a successful artist in the future, good luck on your journey!
